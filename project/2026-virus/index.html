Host defense against viruses

Yu-Hsuan Huang, Ting-Yu Yeh, Huai-Kuang Tsai
Feb 26, 2026

Viruses trigger complex host immune responses, but it remains unclear how different viruses induce distinct antiviral programs and shape cell-cell communication. Using machine learning on transcriptomic data, we seek to identify the characteristic gene programs associated with different viral infections and to map how cells coordinate with one another over the course of infection.

Yu-Hsuan Huang
